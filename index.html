How to Use:

This app is designed for mason brick layers to calculate brick spacings quickly and accurately in conjunction with either a standardized or over size brick spacing ruler. Follow these steps:

  1. Enter the desired height you want to lay to in inches or feet and inches.
  2. Select the spacing size you want to use from the options available.
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to check how well the selected spacing size hits the desired height.
  4. If needed, the app may adjust the spacing size or calculate a remainder for a rip to achieve the desired height.
  5. Lastly, mark out the calculated coursing with your spacing ruler.
